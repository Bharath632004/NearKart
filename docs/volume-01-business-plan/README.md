# Volume 1 – Business Plan (120+ Pages)

## Chapters

1. Executive Summary
   - Vision, Mission, Core Values
   - Problem Statement & Solution
   - Market Opportunity & Company Snapshot

2. Company Profile
   - Founder & Co-Founder Profiles
   - Team Structure & Organizational Chart
   - Legal Structure & Startup India Registration

3. Business Model
   - Business Model Canvas
   - Revenue Model (Commission, Delivery, Subscription, Ads)
   - Merchant & Customer Value Proposition

4. Market & Competitor Analysis
   - TAM / SAM / SOM
   - Competitor Analysis (Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, etc.)
   - SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, PESTLE

5. Marketing Strategy
   - Digital, Social Media, Referral, Offline

6. Operations Plan
   - Merchant Onboarding, Order Flow, Delivery SOPs

7. Financial Report
   - Investment, Revenue Projections, Break-even, ROI

8. Risk & Growth Strategy
   - Risk Mitigation, Funding & Expansion Strategy
